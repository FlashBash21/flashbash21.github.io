Project Overview

If at first you don't succeed, try, try again! In 9 lives, you gain the powers of the enemies who kill you. Starting from nothing, gain melee, ranged, and movement abilities from the rooms beside you. Gain the same ability multiple times to upgrade it. When you are ready, take on the boss! But be careful, you have only 9 lives!

9 Lives was developed with the Video Game Developement Club at the University of Minnesota over a single semester. I led a team of 2 other students students in the programming and software design of 9 Lives. I designed UML to plan and streamline developement for the group. Additionally, I utilized object-oriented design patterns such as the Decorator pattern for the modular ability system.

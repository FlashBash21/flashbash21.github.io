Project Overview

Lyin' Jack is 2D card game with mechanics taken heavily from the classic casino game Blackjack. In Lyin' Jack, the dealer is allowed to change the rules of the game, creating interesting situations and strategy much different from typical casino Blackjack.

Lyin' Jack was created for the 2025 Gamecrafters Summer Game Jam in a team of 6. My personal contributions were to the design of the original concept, and programing the card and deck logic, as well as the betting system. Lyin' Jack went on to win the jam.
